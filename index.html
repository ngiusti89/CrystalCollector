Crystal Collector

How to Play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
The value of each crystal is hidden and can only be discovered by clicking on them.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random Number to Get.
If you go over the Number to get, you lose.
At the end of each game a new number will be regenerated, and your wins/losses will be added to the tally.

Good Luck!
